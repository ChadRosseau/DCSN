About Us

Our Mission

Discovery College Student News (DCSN) is the official student-led newspaper of Discovery College. We here at DCSN aim to educate the DC student populace on contemporary global and local issues. By developing unique and engaging content, we hope to arm our peers with relevant information they can actively use in their academic and social lives.
Every month, we at DCSN create content around important topics relating to one of the seventeen UN sustainable development goals. Under our four categories of poverty, sustainability, economy, and politics, lie all seventeen UN sustainable development goals. There you can find content in all types of mediums and on all types of IB and MYP relevant issues.
Through our posts, we strive to raise the cultural and international awareness of DC students, increasing their understanding of global issues by giving them real-life examples of the concepts they learn in class. This, in turn, may help our readers in their studies moving forward, allowing them to better engage in classes.
Our Beliefs

We at DCSN believe in promoting greater academic engagement at DC by fostering a culture of international mindedness beyond the classroom.

Our People

Participation in DCSN is open to all secondary and Diploma Program students (Y7-13). Additionally, our members possess a wide variety of skills and specialties across artistic disciplines.

Our Values

From a young age, IB students are taught to think critically about the world around us. We at DCSN value this approach and look to extend it to international contexts.
