Logging into the DCSN website will provide some additional features for those using Discovery College (@dc.edu.hk) emails, such as being able to see more information on student staff members.

Feel free to contact us if you have any questions or concerns.

Please note:

By logging into the DCSN website, you are consenting to storage of some personal information. This information includes:

  • The display name tied to your Google account
  • Your Google account's profile picture
  • Your Google email address

All authentication is handled by externally by Google's GAuth API. We do not store usernames or passwords in our database.
